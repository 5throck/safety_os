## tar-planning

> **Status**: Stub — implementation required

### Purpose

Plan and coordinate turnaround (TAR) shutdown operations for chemical plants, ensuring safe execution of maintenance, inspection, and modification activities during plant shutdown periods.

### Scope

- Pre-TAR risk assessment and hazard identification
- PSSR (Pre-Startup Safety Review) coordination
- Contractor surge management and onboarding
- Safe work planning (hot work, confined space, elevated work)
- Turnaround timeline and milestone tracking
- Post-TAR startup verification

### Legal Basis

| Law | Article | Requirement |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| 산업안전보건법 | Article 34 | 안전보건관리체계 확립 |
| 산업안전보건법 | Article 44 | 공정안전관리 (Process Safety Management) |
| 안전보건규칙 | Articles 196-204 | 작업장 안전점검 |
