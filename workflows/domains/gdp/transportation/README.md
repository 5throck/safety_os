# transportation Workflow

## 1. Objective
Manage pharmaceutical transportation with proper vehicles, route tracking, and temperature control per KGDP.

## 2. Applicability
All pharmaceutical shipments between GDP facilities and to customers.

## 3. Workflow Steps
1. **Vehicle qualification**: Verify transport company GDP compliance.
2. **Packaging selection**: Insulated containers, gel packs for cold chain.
3. **Route planning**: Optimize for time and temperature stability.
4. **Pre-shipment temperature check**: Verify product temperature before loading.
5. **In-transit monitoring**: Temperature logger active during transit.
6. **Delivery confirmation**: Customer signature, condition check, DTS scan.
7. **Post-shipment logger analysis**: Verify no excursion during transit.

## 4. Evidence Record
Generate `gdp-transportation-record.json` with route and temperature data.

## 5. KPI
- 100% on-time delivery
- <0.1% in-transit temperature excursion
