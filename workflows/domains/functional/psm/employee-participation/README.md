
# Employee Participation Program (Element 13)

## Overview
This workflow defines the Employee Participation Program for covered processes in compliance with OSHA-KR Article 44. Employers must develop a written plan of action regarding the implementation of employee participation, and workers have the right to participate in the development and conduct of PHAs, incident investigations, compliance audits, and procedure reviews.

## Scope
All workers assigned to covered processes, including contract workers where applicable, are entitled to participate in PSM-related activities. Management must actively facilitate participation opportunities and respond to employee recommendations.

## Procedure

### 1. Participation Activity Types
Workers may participate in the following PSM activities, each of which generates an evidence record:
- **PHA Participation**: Contributing to Process Hazard Analysis or HAZOP studies.
- **Incident Review**: Participating in incident investigation teams.
- **Audit Participation**: Joining compliance audit teams or walk-throughs.
- **Procedure Review**: Reviewing and providing feedback on Standard Operating Procedures.
- **Safety Committee**: Attending PSM-related safety committee meetings.

### 2. Worker Rights Under OSHA-KR Article 44
- Workers have the right to be informed of PHAs and to review PHA findings applicable to their work area.
- Workers have the right to participate in incident investigations involving their process or work area.
- Workers have the right to access process safety information, operating procedures, and audit results.

### 3. Recording Participation
- Record the activity type, date, PSM element discussed, and the worker IDs of all participants.
- Capture all recommendations submitted by workers during the activity.
- Record management's formal response to each recommendation and the implementation status.

### 4. Recommendation Follow-Up
- Management must provide a written response to worker recommendations within a defined timeframe (recommended: 30 days).
- Track implementation status for each recommendation through to closure.
- Retain records as evidence of active employee participation for PSM compliance audits.
