
# Contractor Management Procedure

## Overview
This procedure outlines the process for managing contractor safety in accordance with OSHA-KR Article 44 (Process Safety Management) for chemical processing facilities.

## 1. Safety Plan Review
- Contractors must submit a safety plan prior to beginning work.
- The safety plan must be reviewed and approved by the Safety Workflow Manager or designated EHS personnel.

## 2. Pre-Work Safety Training
- All contractor workers must complete site-specific pre-work safety training.
- Training records must be verified and logged in the system.

## 3. Contractor Evaluation
- Contractors will be evaluated based on safety performance, compliance, and incident history.
- Evaluation scores will be maintained in the contractor safety record.
