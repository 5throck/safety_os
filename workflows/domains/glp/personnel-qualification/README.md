# personnel-qualification Workflow

## 1. Objective
Maintain personnel qualification and training records per OECD GLP Section 2.

## 2. Applicability
All personnel involved in GLP studies (Study Director, Principal Investigators, technicians, QAU).

## 3. Workflow Steps
1. **Job description**: Clearly defined role, responsibilities.
2. **Education/experience records**: CV, training certificates on file.
3. **GLP training**: Initial and annual refresh.
4. **Role-specific training**: Equipment operation, SOP familiarization.
5. **Competency assessment**: Periodic verification.
6. **Health surveillance**: Per occupational-health-agent coordination.

## 4. Key Roles
- **Study Director**: Ultimate responsibility for study (per OECD GLP Section 1.2)
- **Principal Investigator**: For multi-site studies
- **QAU Personnel**: Independent of study team
- **Test Facility Management**: Overall facility responsibility

## 5. Evidence Record
Generate `glp-personnel-record.json` with training history.
