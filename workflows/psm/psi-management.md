# Process Safety Information (PSI) Management Workflow

## Purpose
To ensure accurate collection, registration, and updating of Process Safety Information (PSI) in compliance with OSHA-KR Article 44.

## Scope
Includes piping and instrumentation diagrams (P&IDs), material safety data sheets (MSDS), process chemistry, maximum intended inventory, safe upper and lower limits, and evaluation of consequences of deviations.

## Process Steps

1. **Information Collection**
   - Gather all chemical hazard information (MSDS).
   - Collect process technology information (block flow diagrams, process chemistry, inventory).
   - Compile equipment design information (P&IDs, materials of construction, electrical classification).

2. **Verification & Registration**
   - Review information for accuracy and completeness.
   - Register the PSI documents into the central EHS document management system.

3. **Approval & Distribution**
   - Route PSI documentation to Process Engineering and EHS leads for approval.
   - Distribute approved PSI to operations and maintenance teams.

4. **Periodic Review & Update**
   - Update PSI whenever there are changes in the process (MOC workflow).
   - Conduct a comprehensive review of PSI every 5 years or per internal policy.
