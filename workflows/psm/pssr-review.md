# Pre-Startup Safety Review (PSSR)

## Overview
This workflow defines the procedure for conducting a Pre-Startup Safety Review (PSSR) to ensure safety readiness before the initial startup of new or modified facilities, in accordance with Process Safety Management (PSM) and Management of Change (MOC) procedures.

## Procedure

1. **PSSR Trigger**
   - Initiated when a new process facility is constructed.
   - Initiated when a modification (MOC) is completed and ready for startup.

2. **Team Assembly**
   - Form a PSSR team consisting of operations, maintenance, safety, and engineering personnel.

3. **Checklist Execution**
   - Verify that construction and equipment are in accordance with design specifications.
   - Ensure safety, operating, maintenance, and emergency procedures are in place and adequate.
   - Verify that a Process Hazard Analysis (PHA) has been performed for new facilities and recommendations have been resolved or implemented before startup.
   - Ensure training of each employee involved in operating a process has been completed.

4. **Deficiency Resolution**
   - Any critical safety items identified must be resolved before startup.
   - Non-critical items may be scheduled for post-startup resolution with appropriate mitigating measures.

5. **Approval**
   - Final review and sign-off by the PSSR team lead and facility manager.
   - Authorization for startup.

6. **Documentation**
   - Record the PSSR using the `psm-pssr-record` evidence model.
   - Store records in the compliance audit trail.
