# Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) Management

## Overview
This workflow defines the management of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) as required by OSHA-KR Article 44.

## 1. SOP Drafting
- Draft SOPs based on process safety information and hazard analysis.
- Ensure all safety limits and required personal protective equipment (PPE) are specified.

## 2. SOP Revision
- Revise SOPs whenever there is a change in the process, equipment, or safety guidelines.
- Track version changes and maintain an audit trail of all revisions.

## 3. Distribution and Acknowledgment
- Distribute the updated SOP to all relevant workers.
- Collect acknowledgment signatures from workers confirming they have read and understood the SOP.
